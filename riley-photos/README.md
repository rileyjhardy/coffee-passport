# Riley Photos

**Photos needed:**

- `happy.jpg` - Your happy/excited face (used for positive verdicts) ✅
- `sad.jpg` - Your sad/disappointed face (used for negative verdicts) ✅
- `thinking.jpg` or `thinking.gif` - Your thinking face (used during the "consulting" animation) ✅

Image recommendations:
- Keep them under 500KB for fast loading
- Square aspect ratio works best (will be displayed as 60x60px circles)
- JPG, PNG, or GIF format
